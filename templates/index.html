How the AI Engine Works
👷
Occupation Risk

🌦
Weather Simulation
Real-time weather risk per region. Monsoon seasons, extreme heat, and storms adjust premiums automatically.

Age Factor
Workers under 25 and over 50 carry adjusted premiums, reflecting actuarial risk curves.
Premium Formula
Final Premium = Base × Occupation Factor × Age Factor × Weather Risk Multiplier
Example: ₹5,000 (construction) × 1.40 × 1.00 × 1.20 (heavy rain) = ₹8,400 / year
